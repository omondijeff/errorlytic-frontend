# Hero Image Instructions

## Adding Your Car Image

To complete the landing page design, add your VW car hero image to this directory:

**File Name:** `hero-car.jpg`
**Full Path:** `/frontend/public/images/hero-car.jpg`

## Image Specifications

- **Recommended Size:** 1920x1080px or higher
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or wider
- **Subject:** VW vehicle (preferably Tiguan or similar model)
- **Lighting:** Studio lighting with spotlight from above works best
- **Background:** Dark or black background recommended
- **Quality:** High resolution for sharp display on retina screens

## Design Notes

The image will be used as a full-screen background with:
- Spotlight effect overlay from top
- Ground reflection/shadow effect at bottom
- Dark gradient from left for text readability
- Text overlaid on the left side

## Current State

Until you add the image, a gradient placeholder will be shown as the background.

## Quick Test

After adding the image, refresh your browser at http://localhost:5173 to see it in action!
